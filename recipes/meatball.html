Meatball

Home

Description

My favorite baked meatball recipe, it's easy to make, full of great flavor, and incredibly versatile.
Serve it with marinara sauce, Swedish cream sauce, American BBQ and beyond

Ingredients

  1. Ground beef
  2. Egg
  3. Parmesan Cheese
  4. Onion
  5. Garlic
  6. Salt Pepper
  7. Parsley

Steps

  • Place all ingredients in a large bowl and stir untill combined
  • Scoop the meat from the bowl and form into 1 1/2 balls. Then place on baking sheet
  • Place onto prepared baking sheet and bake meatballs uncovered for 17-20 minutes. flip halfway through
  • If desired, simmer with marinara sauce for 10 minutes and serve over cooked pasta
