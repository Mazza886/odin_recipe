Fried Rice

Home

Description

This recipe gives the bones for delicious, classic fried rice, yet it also a great starting point to making a variation of your own

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Stir-fry garlic, scallion whites, peas, and carrots in a large wok or nonstick frying pan
  2. Add the rice and add seasoning
  3. Push the fried rice to one side of the pan, then add your eggs to the other side
  4. Drizzle with sesame oil and garnish with scallion greens
