Pancake


Description

This pancake recipe was a total winner.
They're quick and easy to make full of those nonstalgic comforting pancake flavors we all love.

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • granulated (white) sugar
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • butter
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • maple syrup

Steps

  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  2. In a separate medium whisk together melted butter, eggs and vanilla
  3. Combine them and let the batter sit at room temperature for 10 minutes to thicken up
  4. Preheat a griddle pan over medium-high heat
  5. Grease the griddle or skillet with a generous pat of butter
  6. pour the batter in a 1/2 cup dollops on the hot griddle
  7. cook the pancakes until bubbles begin to pop on the top and edges are set, about 3 minutes
  8. use the spatula to flip and cook for another 2 or 3 minutes until golden brown
  9. serve the pancakes stacked high with plenty of maple syrup
